# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Astute is for real estate investors, operators, and acquisition teams who need to evaluate small multifamily opportunities across multiple markets. They are comparing deals, reading market signals, pressure-testing underwriting, and deciding which assets deserve deeper diligence.

## Product Purpose

Astute turns maps, property data, portfolio context, underwriting strategy, and market insight into one real estate intelligence product. The homepage should sell the product by demonstrating how a user moves from geographic signal to investment decision.

## Brand Personality

Precise, cinematic, institutional. Astute should feel like a serious investment terminal with Apple-level polish, not a generic SaaS dashboard or playful proptech demo.

## Anti-references

Avoid toy-like gradients, fake 3D landmarks, generic startup cards, basic dashboard screenshots, overdecorated glassmorphism, and school-project map demos. Do not depend on Mac-only native packages or reintroduce the deleted fake 3D landmark overlay.

## Design Principles

1. Show the product behaving, not just describing itself.
2. Treat maps, metrics, and property cards as one operating system.
3. Use cinematic motion only when it explains focus, hierarchy, or decision flow.
4. Keep financial data clear enough for repeated professional use.
5. Preserve existing routes and app functionality while making the brand feel premium.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect reduced motion preferences, keep text contrast high on dark glass surfaces, preserve keyboard-accessible navigation, and ensure mobile layouts avoid overlap or hidden controls.
